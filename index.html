save the bees

Bees are the world's most common pollinators and they are currently dying out due to Colony Collapse Disorder. They play a crucial role in the ecosystem and food production. Honey bees are responsible for $30 billion a year in crops (BBC, 2023) and according to AP News (2023), 48% of honeybee colonies in the United States of America were lost in the past year.

  • Bees produce over 66% edible crops
  • They pollinate a diverse range of plants that maintain the stability of the ecosystem
  • They help ecosystems withstand climate change and environmental changes
